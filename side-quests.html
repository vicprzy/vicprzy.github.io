Audio Production

Creating electronic music and exploring sound design through various DAWs and synthesizers.

Music Production Electronic

Music Production Journey

I've been producing music for 8 years, starting with basic beat-making and evolving into complex composition and sound design. My work spans multiple genres including ambient, electronic, and experimental music.

I use Ableton Live as my primary DAW, supplemented by various VSTs and hardware synthesizers. My production process emphasizes experimentation and pushing the boundaries of conventional sound design.

Notable Works

  • Released 3 EPs on independent labels
  • Collaborated with 10+ artists on remixes and original compositions
  • Performed at 15+ electronic music venues and festivals
  • Created soundtracks for 5 indie game projects
Personal Reflection: Music production is my creative outlet where I can express emotions and ideas that words alone cannot capture. It's a meditative process that requires both technical skill and artistic intuition.
