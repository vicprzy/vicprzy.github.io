About This Hobby

I started photography during college as a way to explore the world around me. What began as casual snapshots has evolved into a serious passion for capturing authentic moments and telling stories through images.

My work focuses on natural lighting, composition, and capturing genuine human emotions. I've had the privilege of traveling to 15+ countries and documenting diverse cultures and landscapes.

Personal Reflection: Photography has taught me patience, observation, and the importance of perspective. It's a constant reminder that beauty exists everywhere if we take the time to look for it.

Music Production

Music Production Journey

I've been producing music for 8 years, starting with basic beat-making and evolving into complex composition and sound design. My work spans multiple genres including ambient, electronic, and experimental music.

I use Ableton Live as my primary DAW, supplemented by various VSTs and hardware synthesizers. My production process emphasizes experimentation and pushing the boundaries of conventional sound design.

Notable Works

  • Released 3 EPs on independent labels
  • Collaborated with 10+ artists on remixes and original compositions
  • Performed at 15+ electronic music venues and festivals
  • Created soundtracks for 5 indie game projects
Personal Reflection: Music production is my creative outlet where I can express emotions and ideas that words alone cannot capture. It's a meditative process that requires both technical skill and artistic intuition.

Rock Climbing

Challenging myself both physically and mentally through indoor and outdoor rock climbing.

Climbing Sports Outdoor

Climbing Adventures

Rock climbing has become more than just a hobby—it's a lifestyle. I started at an indoor gym 6 years ago and have since progressed to outdoor climbing at various crags and climbing destinations.

My climbing journey has taken me to incredible locations including Yosemite, Red Rock Canyon, and climbing areas across Europe. Each climb teaches me about perseverance, problem-solving, and overcoming fear.

Climbing Achievements

  • Lead climbing grade: 5.11d
  • Bouldering grade: V6
  • Completed multi-pitch routes in 5 countries
  • Mentored 20+ beginner climbers
Personal Reflection: Climbing has taught me that limits are often self-imposed. The mental aspect of climbing—trusting your body, managing fear, and pushing through doubt—has profoundly impacted how I approach challenges in all areas of life.
