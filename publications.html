Nutrition

Do you remember the days of "eating chocolate helps with period cramps" or "don't eat certain fatty foods during menstration"? Well, here we'll be recapping some suggestions from Forbes magazine relating to what foods you should prioritize at different stages!

Foods to eat during the Follicular Phase

During the Follicular Phase, a woman's estrogen levels start to rise. This rise in estrogen leads to cravings and impacts her hydration levels. It is necessary for women to be listening to these cravings! This is their body telling them what they need, but it's also important to look out for what they're eating.

Some recommeneded foods to savor the cravings:

- Carbohydrates such as bread or pasta 🥐

- Complex carbohydrates like whole grains, fruits, or starchy vegetables 🥔 (potatoes or yams)

- Lots of liquids, especially water

Foods to eat during the Ovulation Phase

In the course of the Ovulation Phase, a woman's body is getting ready to release an egg. The body starts to need antioxidants as well as fatty acids to support the egg's travel out of the ovaries.

Some recommended foods for this time:

- Walnuts, salmon, tuna, flaxseed, and chia seeds 🥏 (all high in fatty acids)

- Kale, blackberries, blueberries, and spinach 🥗 (all high in antioxidants)

Foods to eat during the Luteal Phase

At this point, a woman's body is starting to prepare the uterus for a potential pregnancy. It is vital for lots of nutrients to be supplied, including fats and protein. During this time, it is recommeneded to not skip any meals especially with the occurances of mood swings, constant nutrition helps with maintaining blood sugar levels too. Some women also start craving pre-menstral phase, so it's important to listen to this cravings as well!

Some recommended foods for this time:

- Avocado, fish, nuts 🥑 (all high in protein and good-fats)

- White beans, brussel sprouts, broccoli, and tofu 🥦 (high in calcium)

- Pumpkin seeds, cashews, and dark chocolate 🍫 (high in magnesium)

- Lentils, chickpeas, spinach, oats, and silver beets 🌱 (high in iron)

Foods to eat during the Menstral Phase

Throughout the Menstral Phase, the uterine lining sheds. At this time, a lot of iron and blood is lost through this shedding and it is then vital to regenerate as much nutrients as possible to make up for what was lost.

Some recommended foods for this time:

- Turkey, lean beef, and bean 🍗 (protein that is high in iron)

- Nuts and dark chocolate 🥜 (snacks that are high in iron)
